Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Thandi and Sipho, Ubuntu Coffee House blends township vibes with specialty coffee. We source beans from women-owned co-ops in Ethiopia and local roasteries in Durban.

Our name reflects the African philosophy of togetherness - "I am because we are."
